Meet the Main Building Blocks

Protons and neutrons are built from two main types of quarks. Each has a different electrical charge.

+

Up Quark

The Up quark has a positive electrical charge. Think 'up' like a thumbs-up for positivity!

-

Down Quark

The Down quark has a negative electrical charge. It's the opposite of the Up quark.

The Quark Family

Besides Up and Down, there are four other types (or "flavors") of quarks, but they are much rarer and heavier. Their names are Charm, Strange, Top, and Bottom. For now, we only need Up and Down to build almost everything in the universe!
